Audio Samples of the paper "Cross-lingual Speaker Adaptation using Domain Adaptation and Speaker Consistency Loss for Text-To-Speech Synthesis"

Authors: Detai Xin, Yuki Saito, Shinnosuke Takamichi, Tomoki Koriyama, Hiroshi Saruwatari

Note: In order to obtain best quality, we strongly encourage the listeners to take their headphones.

Contents

  • Intra-lingual TTS synthesis
  • Cross-lingual TTS synthesis

 

We used the combination of VCTK and JVS as training datasets. They included 107 English and 100 Japanese speakers. We used VCTK & JVS to train the speaker encoder and VCTK to train the TTS model. We randomly picked up 8 Japanese and 8 English speakers as unseen speaker. Any data of unseen speakers were excluded from the training dataset. Note that, for TTS model the seen speakers were not seen by the two models without using SCL (Base. and DA).

All of the utterances below are not included in training data.
